Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I browse products?

Use the categories (Topwear, Bottomwear, Footwear, Accessories, Fragrances) to explore our collection.

2. How can I check product prices?

Each product card shows the name, image, and price at the bottom. Click on a product for detailed info.

3. Do I need an account to buy?

No, you can browse freely. Checkout will be available soon. Creating an account allows faster checkout and order tracking.

4. How do I contact support?

You can email us at support@trendettic.com or call +91 93253 90860 during business hours.

5. How do I track my order?

Once checkout is available, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor delivery status.

6. What is your return policy?

We currently accept exchanges within 14 days of purchase. Ensure the product is unused and in original packaging.

7. Can I subscribe to newsletters?

Yes! Subscribe on our Enquiry Form page to receive updates, offers, and fashion tips directly in your inbox.
